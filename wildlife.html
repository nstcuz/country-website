Azores Wildlife

From rare birds like the Azores bullfinch in lush forests to giants like the blue whale in surrounding waters, this archipelago is a unique blend of terrestrial and marine marvels. Endemic species and breathtaking landscapes make the Azores a compact yet diverse haven, inviting exploration and calling for conservation efforts.
